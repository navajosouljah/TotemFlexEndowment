The Problem with Traditional Endowments

You built something
that matters.
Your capital should too.

Most donors don't ask the right questions about endowments. The structure depends on it.

Here's what you were trained to accept. Your capital is given away forever. Your nonprofit choice is permanent. Your impact is measured by an annual letter. The fees that come out before the giving begins are unavoidable. And when the markets fall, your generosity falls with them.

Here's what Totem's FlexEndowment offers instead. Your capital is preserved, not surrendered. If you ever need it, you are never more than 90 days away from your money. Your nonprofit choice is yours to change whenever you want. Your impact is measured in cash distributions every 90 days, not market-dependent estimates on an annual letter.

And the math is not close. Traditional endowments distribute up to 7% annually to their nonprofits. Totem distributes 15%. The difference is not a marketing claim. It is structural. We fund insured commercial transactions instead of paying ourselves a management fee. Nobody is collecting in the middle, so the giving doesn't have to be cut in half before it reaches the cause.
